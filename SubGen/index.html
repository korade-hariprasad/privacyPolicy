Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21 May 2025

This application is designed to work completely offline on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All subtitle files and audio/video content you process are stored only locally on your device and never uploaded or shared.

However, to enable AI-powered translation and transliteration features, the app downloads an AI model from the internet. This download is the only instance of network usage and does not involve sending any of your personal data or content.

Your privacy and data security are our highest priority while using this app.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at hariprasadkorade@gmail.com.
